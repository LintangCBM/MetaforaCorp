Metafora Corp

Metafora Corp is a dynamic and fast-growing holding company in Indonesia with a diverse business portfolio. Established to drive innovation and excellence, Metafora Corp actively operates across a range of industries, including cutting-edge technology, real estate and property development, and retail. With a strong commitment to growth and sustainability, the company continues to expand its reach and impact, making positive contributions to the Indonesian economy and beyond.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be Indonesia's leading business enterprise, setting new standards in advancing business knowledge and promoting ethical and sustainable practices. We aim to inspire innovation, drive growth, and lead in creating a future where businesses thrive responsibly by prioritising the well-being of people and the environment.

Our Mission

At Metafora Corp, our mission is to provide exceptional service to all our partners and stakeholders. We strive to build long-term relationships based on trust, integrity and mutual success. By prioritising excellence and innovation, we ensure that our actions have a positive impact not only on our business ecosystem, but also on society as a whole.

Tagline

To reinforce our vision and mission, we proudly use our tagline: 'Empowering You!' This phrase summarises our commitment to empowering individuals, businesses and communities. Whether through our innovative technology, high-quality real estate solutions or retail experience, we aim to enhance and support our clients, partners and stakeholders in achieving their goals and aspirations.
